About

Name : Tijwet (from Haitian Creole)

Creator : Jonathan Sanon

Website : https://tijwet.com

What It's About

Tijwet is a growing collection of quick, easy-to-play games — perfect for spontaneous fun with friends and family, anytime, anywhere. No downloads. No distractions. Just games that work right on the screen in front of you.

Why It Exists

I wanted a simple space to play together on the same screen, whether it’s during a break, at a gathering, or just because. Tijwet brings back the joy of lightweight, classic-style games made to be shared.

What's Coming

More games, better accessibility, and maybe even new ways to play with others on the same device. This is just the start — the goal is to build a platform that feels playful and personal at the same time.
